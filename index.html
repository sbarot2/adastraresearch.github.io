About Us

Ad Astra Research is a consulting and tools company focused on validating and verifying emerging technologies across energy, instrumentation, and advanced research environments. We bring real-world skepticism, system-level design, and modern AI tooling to help teams verify what's real and reproducible.

Ad Astra DAQ

We build open-source, modular DAQ hardware (Ethernet/PoE powered) with Python-based control software. Our system supports RS-232, RS-485, thermocouples, 4–20mA, ADCs, relays, digital I/O and more.
